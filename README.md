# Consulting-Case-Books

📊 Consulting Case Books – Global Collection

A curated repository of consulting case books from top business schools and consulting clubs worldwide to help you master case interviews, structured thinking, and business problem-solving.

📌 Overview

This repository brings together high-quality consulting case books from leading institutions across the world. These case books are widely used by candidates preparing for roles in:

💼 Management Consulting (McKinsey, BCG, Bain, etc.)
📦 Product Management
📊 Data Analytics / Data Science
📈 Business Strategy & Operations

Each case book typically includes:

Real case interview questions
Step-by-step case-solving approaches
Standard consulting frameworks
Guesstimates and market sizing problems
Industry-specific business cases
